ABOUT EXPRESSO

"Literature and Art are the Finite Expressions of the Infinite Mind"

Expresso was formed back in 2019 with a vision to celebrate the infinite mind. Expresso came up as an amalgamation of both Literary and Arts Clubs of NIT Patna and brought them together, 'Infinite Ideas, One Place'.

We believe in bringing out one's true creative self and building the courage to stand among all. Guided and supported by our Dean Student Welfare, Dr. Prakash Chandra and also our Professor In charge, Dr. Khursheed Ahmad, and not to mention the sincere efforts of the founding members and the team we have grown up and are still rising.
